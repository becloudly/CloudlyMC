DESIGN ELEMENTS LEGEND
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Symbols:
  ⚙  - Administration/settings
  📋 - Whitelist/management
  🔗 - Discord/connections
  ℹ  - Information/help
  ▪  - Bullet point
  │  - Vertical separator
  ↳  - Continuation/description arrow
  ✓  - Success indicator
  ✗  - Error indicator
  ⏳ - Loading/wait indicator
  ⚠  - Warning indicator

Colors (Minecraft codes):
  §6§l - Bold Gold (Headers)
  §e§l - Bold Yellow (Section headers)
  §a§l - Bold Green (Success)
  §c§l - Bold Red (Errors)
  §6§l - Bold Yellow (Warnings)
  §f   - White (Primary text)
  §e   - Yellow (Highlighted values)
  §7   - Gray (Descriptions)
  §8   - Dark Gray (Decorative)
  §b   - Aqua (Links)
  §8§m - Dark Gray Strikethrough (Separator lines)
